About the Cavapoo

Say Hello to your new puppy, the Cavapoo! The Cavapoo is a mix betweem the Cavalier King Charles Spaniel and the poodle. These dogs are playful, good with children, intelligent, and are super easy to train. They can weigh anywhere between 8-25 lbs and live around 10-15 years. They are low shedding dogs which make them great family pets. Cavapoos can rage in many different colors some being a light golden color, white, black, brown, white, or sme other combination. The Cavapoo is a very inteligent breed which can be a good and bad thing. For example, it makes new tricks, and trainig easy, but they leran quick, even the things you don't want them to learn, like jumping on the table, so keep this in mind.
