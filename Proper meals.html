To keep your Cavapoo healthy make sure to check for...

  • Feed according to their weight: Make sure you've consulted a vet, or breeder about your cavapoos weight. This prevents under or over feeding.
  • Feed according to their engery/activity level: Depending on how physically active your cavapoo is this can change the amount of food they get.
  • Avoid processed food: Avoid food like kibble; kibble is packed with perservatives that can slowly decline their health overtime. Use foods like raw meat or other high protein foods.
  • Allergies: Allergies in Cavapoos are very common some include all types of meat, and can clause itchy skin, constant ear infections, vomiting, diarrhea, and gastrointestinal discomfort.
  • Age: If your Cavapoo is still a puppy, start with small portions of food, and slowly increase over time.
