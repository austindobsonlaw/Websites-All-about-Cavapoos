When you first bring your Cavapoo home

There are many things to consider when bring home a cavapoo for the first time. For example, they are like todlers, because in their minds they are. This means that any thing droped, or left on the ground could be chewed and possibly swallowed. To prevent this from happening your job is before, or just as it gets home, make sure that there is nothing on the ground for your cavapoo to chew. If they are still a puppy then they will be teething and go around chewing up everything. Id this is the case then make sure to put them in an area with no places or things to chew up, or in an easily supervised area.

How to prevent your cavapoo from chewing furniture or yourself

  • Lay out an area where the furniture can't be chewed with gates up
  • Supervise them closley at the start, and slowley let them have more independence
  • Tell them no when they start chewing furniture or you
  • Praise them when you tell them no, and they listen
