How to train your Cavapoo!

Training puppies have recieved mixed expeiences when training puppies. Fortunatley, for you the cavapoo is a very intelligent dog which makes training them new things much easier. However, they also tend to learn things that you don't want them to know, for example jumping up on the table to steal food if you leave a chair out, finding ways out of their assigned space, and playing and tearing at things that aren't their toys, and manipulating you to give them food to name a few.

Here are some ways to teach your cvapoo new tricks!

  • Make sure to praise them and give them lots of treats if they preform the action, they will associate it with getting a treat
  • Start off easy, teach them tricks like sit, or lie down
  • Don't scold them for not doing the action, training requires patience
  • Slowley work your way up to more complicated tricks like, shake a paw, or roll over
  • Remember to take your time, they're learning. Think of it like learning a new language
